Add another security layer to your crypto assets

Cerberus Wallet is open source project which adds two factor authentification for your crypto wallet. Even if your private key would be stolen, hacker would not be able to transfer your assets without your confirmation.

New level of security

2FA protection for your transactions
Protect your cryptoassets with two factor authentification. Each transaction must be confirmed from your smartphone in order to be executed.
Your cryptoassets under 24/7 online control
When someone send signed transaction to your wallet, you'll get get push notification immediately. You could cancel this transaction and have an ability to transfer your money to safe place.
ChainLink technology
Cerberus wallet uses ChainLink oracles technology, which guarantees that your smartcontract would be able to check your 2FA confirmation.

Feature Highlights

Confirm each transaction

To complete a transaction, you must consciously confirm the transaction using the Cerberus Wallet mobile app. If you have few devices, you can set a minimum number of confirmations for the transaction. If at least one device cancels a transaction, it will be canceled.

Control your wallet 24/7

Receive notifications of every signed transaction. Receiving a notification of an unauthorized signed transaction indicates that your private key has been hacked, so transfer your assets to another account / wallet as soon as possible.

Add new wallet in one-click

To add a new wallet to your application, simply scan the QR code and the application will do the rest.

Panic mode (soon)

Block all confirmations for 24 hours if you have a weak internet connection and a fraud problem is detected. Rest assured that no one will complete the transaction within the next 24 hours.

Friends unlock (experimental, soon)

Experimental feature which allows you to sign a transaction if your selected 5 friends publish secret code on their social networks. It helps people who lost their private key. This transaction must be confirmed using Cerberus Wallet app and could be canceled for 24 hours from signing.
